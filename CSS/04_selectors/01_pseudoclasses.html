What is Pseudo Classes ?

Ans : used to add styles to selectors, but only when those selectors meet certain conditions. A pseudo class is expressed by adding a colon (:) after a selector in CSS, followed by a pseudo-class such as "hover", "focus", or "active".


Pseudo Classes
  • :active
  • :any-link
  • :autofill
  • :after
  • :before
  • :hover
  • :checked
  • :focus
  • :visited
